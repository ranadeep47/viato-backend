FAQ

All you need to know about us.

How does it work?

Choose your next book to read using a simple search. Place the order. We will deliver the book to you in 48hrs to your doorstep and collect the cash.

How long can i keep the book?

30 days. If thats not enough you can extend for 30 more days with a small extension fee. If it takes forever you can always return the book to pick another.

How many books can i rent at once?

You can rent upto a maximum of 2 books at once. If you want to order another book just return the ones you've finished reading.

What if I dont return the book?

We belive book readers are literate and share our mission of spreading the book reading joy to others.

Are there any subscription plans?

No, you are not tied up to some montly subscription plan which you may not use. In this on-demand era, we feel books can be rented on-demand too.

Is there any delivery charge?

We charge a little amount of ₹30 for delivery.

Can i rent more than 2 books at once?

No, Issue a pickup request for any of the books you've finished reading and then you can place another order.

Do you have the old classics and recent bestsellers ?

Yes! We have a huge inventory of books to choose from to quench your thirst for books.

Can i donate my books ?

If you share our mission that more people should have affordable means to access books, give us your old books and we will distribute them.

What if the book is damaged?

Well, we believe our users handle the books carefully but incase of any damages, an amount equal to rental price will be charged extra.
